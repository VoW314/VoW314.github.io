Experiences

I've been working in internships since the covid-19 pandemic. So far I have been able to work under 3 professors from different institutions as well as have been able to co-author a paper in 2023.

2023 Paper

Co-authored a paper within my undergraduate researcher job. The paper was a study of using different network changes to deter a malicious agent. After this was published, we were able to present this research at UVA SIEDS 2023 and HammerCon 2023.

I like explaining this using one of Sun Tzu's quotes in the Art of War: "If you know the enemy and know yourself, you need not fear the result of a hundred battles." We are aiming to know our enemy in this paper. As the blue team we know ourselves. This information will allow to win our "hundred battles".

Hume Center Research

During the first semester at Virginia Tech, I was able to get a job as an undergraduate-researcher. I have been working here during the semesters since 2021. I also worked here during the summer during the 2021 internship.

The research is focused in machine learning and cybersecurity. Specifically using reinforcement learning to simulate malicious AI and ML agents.

High School Summer Internships

For Dr. Tafti I created a GUI mockup using multiple languages to modernize an older program.
